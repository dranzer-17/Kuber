A single flat card, nothing hidden

Every field is visible at once, grouped by hairline dividers — Organization, People, Batch & Assignment. Closest to how the current form already reads, just tightened up: consistent 2-column field grid, a violet top rail as the only accent, dashed row dividers between people.
